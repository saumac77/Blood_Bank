Under normal circumstances, every two seconds someone needs a blood transfusion. <br>Blood transfusions are used for trauma victims - due to accidents and burns - heart surgery, organ transplants, women with complications during childbirth, newborns and premature babies, and patients receiving treatment for leukemia, cancer or other diseases, such as sickle cell disease and thalassemia.<br>
Also there are many circumstances under which the need of blood is extremely required.
